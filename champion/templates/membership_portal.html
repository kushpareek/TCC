TCC CHAMPION

A full membership to our club is granted to individuals of exceptional talent and capability with a history of demonstrated achievement in multiple aspects of life.

There is no set selection criteria for this coveted membership. Each application is carefully reviewed by our Board and is approved on a voting basis.

Note: This tier of service guarantees access to The Champions Club's Global Private Network.

Applicants are permitted to apply once every month in a calendar year.

Acceptance Rate: <1%
(Last updated 01/09/2024)

TCC ASSOCIATE

An association to our club is granted to individuals who wish to access our array of premium educational content, online resources and much more.

There is no selection criteria for an associate relationship with our club, we welcome students from all parts of the world regardless of age or status.

Note: This tier of service does not provide access to The Champions Club's Global Private Network.

Applicants are permitted to apply once every 24 hours on a daily basis.

Acceptance Rate: N/A
(Last updated 01/09/2024)

TCC PARTNER

A commercial partnership is granted to startup leaders, business owners and senior corporate executives who meet our internal selection criteria.

There is a strict selection criteria for a business relationship with our club, we do not accept any investments into our club and we do not offer equity at all.

Note: This tier of service may provide access to The Champions Club's Global Private Network based on relations.

Applicants are permitted to apply once every 3 months in a calendar year.

Acceptance Rate: 3-12%
(Last updated 01/09/2024)

WE MAKE NO EXCEPTIONS

Individuals Connected With Our Founder

We greatly appreciate your support and excitement for our Founder's vision but we must emphasize that being connected with our Founder (Past/Present) does not grant your application any special consideration under any circumstances.

Please understand that The Champions Club is as exclusive as it gets, our members represent the very best of the best in society. Our Founder has no authority to overrule the club's decision making when it comes to granting membership or partnership.

Family, Staff & Partners

We cherish close relations but it must be noted that being a family member (Immediate/Distant) of our Founder, member of staff (Executive/Workforce) or business partner does not grant your application any special consideration under any circumstances.
