OUR FOUNDER

AN EVERLASTING LEGACY

Graduating from the Harvard Business Analytics Program with the honor of being awarded Distinction at the Harvard Business School, our Founder & Chief Executive Officer Shagun Kalash proved through exceptional personal merit that nothing is impossible as long as there is a will.

Admitted at the age of 26, Mr. Kalash became the youngest student in the history of the Program. Being elected as the youngest Cohort Representative in the history of the Program, Mr. Kalash proved his capabilities as a leader and guide to many on a global level.

Coming from a Service-class family with a long-lost history of Royalty, the blood of Persia's last Knights still flows through our Founder's veins. After a decade of working with Corporate, Government and Private employers, our Founder stood as a self-made man who decided to challenge the extraordinary and take life head on. Shagun Kalash was re-born as a Warrior ready to fight against the Matrix.

With a lifetime of achievements under his belt, Mr. Kalash has vanquished challenges in life with nothing but absolute competence, our Founder's story is a testament to strength and a relentless pursuit of excellence.

"Through thoughts a man develops a Belief, through action a man becomes Capable, through countless battles a man becomes a Warrior, through a lifetime of war a man becomes a Legend, through the stories of his various conquests a man becomes a Myth, through these myths shared over time a man becomes incredible as a Champion." - Shagun Kalash.
