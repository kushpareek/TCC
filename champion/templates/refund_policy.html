Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing The Champions Club. We strive to offer the best possible service and provide valuable educational content. However, we understand that there may be situations where you might seek a refund. This policy outlines the circumstances under which refunds are granted and the process for requesting a refund.

1. No Refunds for Consumed Services

Once a service, such as a video course, has been consumed, it is considered delivered and completed. As such, no refunds will be issued for consumed services. We encourage you to carefully review course descriptions, previews, and any available free content before making a purchase to ensure it meets your expectations.

2. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a refund under the following circumstances:

  • Technical Issues During Payment: If you experience a technical issue during the payment process that results in an overcharge or incorrect billing, please contact our support team immediately. We will investigate the issue and, if a technical error is confirmed, a full refund will be issued.
  • Service Not Provided: If you have paid for a service but it has not been provided to you, please reach out to us with your payment details and a description of the issue. If our team confirms that the service was not delivered as promised, we will issue a full refund.

3. How to Request a Refund

If you believe you are eligible for a refund based on the criteria above, please follow these steps:

  • Contact Us: Email our support team at support@championsclub.com with the subject line "Refund Request". Include your name, email address, order number, and a detailed description of the issue.
  • Investigation: Our team will review your request, investigate the issue, and may contact you for further information if needed. Please allow up to 7 business days for this process.
  • Refund Decision: If your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed using the original payment method. You will receive a confirmation email once the refund has been issued. Please note that it may take additional time for the refund to appear in your account, depending on your bank or payment provider.

4. Exceptions to the Refund Policy

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

  • Change of mind after purchasing a course or content.
  • Failure to complete a course or content within the specified access period.
  • Technical issues related to user hardware, software, or internet connection.
  • Discontent with the content after significant usage.

5. Contact Information

If you have any questions or concerns about our refund policy, please do not hesitate to contact our support team. We are here to assist you and ensure that your experience with The Champions Club is positive and rewarding.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
